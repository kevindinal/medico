Eye care is a specialized field focused on the diagnosis, treatment, and management of conditions affecting the eyes. It includes a wide range of services, from routine eye exams to the treatment of specific eye diseases and conditions. Eye care professionals, such as optometrists and ophthalmologists, provide services to maintain eye health, prevent vision loss, and improve eye function. This can involve the use of various diagnostic tools, prescription of corrective lenses, and treatment for conditions like glaucoma, cataracts, and macular degeneration. The ultimate goal of eye care is to ensure clear, healthy vision and to prevent or treat vision-related problems.
